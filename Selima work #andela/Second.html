Poetry?><

Literature is a subject that tells us the truth about our selves by telling us lies about people that never existed.

See poetry is a part of literature which enables us to express ourselves in a way that the dance of words can only do.

Some of you reading this may have never written one and thats okay. You dont need to be a poet/poetess to love poetry.

Sometimes we find it hard to express ourselves in person and we find that a pen and a piece of paper make us feel at home.

That's not always the case though. It honestly depends on the person.

Other people just like to see life through anothers eyes.

There are diferrent kinds of poems but one version of you.

So as you explore this website find whats suits you and submit your own too. So many people can be inspired by what you write.

But you also have to be careful because sometimes the bricks that build you can also be thrown at you.
